# Output Risk Profile

## Likely Failure Modes

- Research output becomes a generic framework recap instead of a decision-support artifact.
- English version drifts from Chinese claims in bilingual mode.
- `needs verification` claims get polished into facts.
- The output becomes too long to reuse before Smith writing.
- The skill accidentally routes final writing, publishing, or Feishu writes.

## Required Self-Check

Before final response, verify:

- every claim is `sourced`, `inferred`, or `needs verification`
- Chinese is first for Chinese audiences
- bilingual versions mirror claims, sources, confidence scores, and decision
- decision card exists and has a concrete next experiment
- no final post, X Article, Feishu write, publish staging, or publish action was created unless explicitly requested

## Iteration Candidate

Add real outcome evals after three or more `storm-research-agent` runs have been used before actual Smith writing.
