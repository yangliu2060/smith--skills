# Artifact Design Profile

## Artifact Family

Markdown research brief.

## Readability Rules

- Keep section headers domain-specific and decision-oriented.
- Use lists for evidence and contradictions.
- Avoid wide Markdown tables; they render poorly in chat and mobile views.
- Keep bilingual output as two mirrored sections, not interleaved sentence by sentence.
- Put the decision card at the end so a human can act without rereading the whole brief.

## Evidence Hygiene

- Link or name local files for source-backed claims.
- Label uncertain claims as `needs verification`.
- Do not add decorative citations or unsupported authority language.
